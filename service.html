International Patient Care

DH Hospital has a dedicated team to handle the care of international patients within the hospital. This includes coordinating not only the patient’s stay at the hospital and insurance coverage but also airport drop off and pickups as well as hotel accommodation. DH being a JCI-accredited hospital meets international standards in healthcare. With state-of-the-art facilities and a highly skilled team patients are guaranteed a smooth recovery process.
